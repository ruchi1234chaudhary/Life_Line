About Us

Under the Digital India initiative of the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology, Government of India, National Informatics Centre (NIC) has developed the eHospital, e-BloodBank and ORS (Online Registration System) applications.

The eHospital application is the Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) for internal workflows and processes of hospitals. eHospital is a one-stop solution which helps in connecting patients, hospitals and doctors on a single digital platform.
